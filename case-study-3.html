Pushpak Nagar Project

The Challenge:

A residential project in Pushpak Nagar, Navi Mumbai, faced tough competition and limited buyer awareness despite being located near the upcoming Navi Mumbai International Airport. The developer needed a partner who could boost visibility and drive faster sales.

Our Approach:

  • Took over as the sole mandate partner for end-to-end sales management.
  • Conducted market research to identify the right buyer segment for affordable and mid-segment housing.
  • Executed a 360° marketing plan including digital campaigns, channel partner outreach, and on-ground activations.
  • Positioned the project as a lifestyle and investment opportunity in one of Navi Mumbai’s most promising growth corridors.

The Results:

  • Sold 80% of the inventory within 6 months of launch.
  • Expanded reach to buyers across Mumbai, Navi Mumbai, NRIs, and outstation investors.
  • Strengthened the developer’s brand presence in Pushpak Nagar’s emerging market.
