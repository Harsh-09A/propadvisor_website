About PropAdvisor

Our Story

Provide advanced business solutions.

At PropAdvisor, we simplify real estate for developers, families, and investors (including NRIs) in Navi Mumbai. Since 2017, we have been partnering with developers through exclusive mandates to market and sell projects effectively, while also guiding buyers to their dream homes and helping investors make profitable decisions. With strong industry networks, local expertise, and a commitment to transparency, we are more than consultants, we are your trusted property advisors.

We understand that property decisions come with doubts, from project sales challenges for developers to hidden costs and complex paperwork for buyers and investors.
